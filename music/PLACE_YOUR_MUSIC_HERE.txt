HOW TO ADD YOUR VALENTINE'S DAY MUSIC:
========================================

1. Download a Valentine's Day romantic song you like (MP3 format)
2. Rename it to: valentine-song.mp3
3. Place it in this 'music' folder
4. The music will automatically play when you click the music button!

FREE MUSIC RESOURCES:
- YouTube Audio Library (royalty-free)
- Free Music Archive
- Pixabay Music
- Bensound

Note: Make sure the file is named exactly "valentine-song.mp3"
